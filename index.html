Ever since that fateful day in my middle school algebra class, when I first discovered the ‘PRGM’ button on my TI-83, I have been a code junky and tech tinkerer. What started as an endeavor to offload algebra equations from my brain to a microchip sparked a lifelong fascination with development on a multitude of platforms.

From text-based RPGs in TI-BASIC to sci-fi shoot ‘em ups in ActionScript 2, visual-programmed LEGO’s Mindstorms to hand-written C++ on an Arduino Uno, and hobby projects in C# .NET to full-blown reactive web applications made with a full stack of modern web technologies, my life has been punctuated with an infatuation with practically every technology I can get my hands on.

Now, in a tech-world that is expanding and changing in such exciting ways and in such rapid fashion, I am thrilled to take those bookends of my life, the ‘commas’ and ‘periods’ of my hobbyist exploration, and leverage the technologies I have known and loved to create performant, elegant, and responsive application exclamation marks!

I am a full-stack web developer with a passion for all things digital, penning the story of my romance with tech one page, one project at a time.

Let’s write a chapter together.
